Authoritative workflow for handling a correction or rework inside the AgentBoard architecture pipeline — the single source of truth that the /architecture orchestration and the compose stages defer to. Use it whenever architecture output needs substantive rework: a design-review finding to resolve, an owner-requested change to a decision/decomposition/slice, or a failure elsewhere in the pipeline that traces back to architecture. It defines how to source-trace a correction to its real origin in real time (never a fixed problem→stage table), carry it as a declared auditable input into a correction-mode re-run of the right stage, treat the retry cap as a signal to trace outward rather than a hard stop, hand off to the external investigator at the cap, and involve the owner only when the trace lands on the spec. Reach for this even when the user just says a card or plan "keeps failing", "the reviewer flagged something", or "this needs reworking" during an architecture run — don't improvise correction routing without it.
